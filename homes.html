Our Residential Settings

Welcome to Willow House

Willow House is our beautifully presented children's home, offering a nurturing environment designed for comfort, healing, and structured personal progress.

Willow House – 2 Rooms

Home Description

Willow House is a beautifully presented children's home located in a pleasant, semi-rural area. The home boasts a newly decorated, spacious lounge, a modern fully-fitted kitchen and dining room, a large private garden, and fully personalised bedrooms for each resident. Staff sleep-in facilities are fully integrated.

Contact Sahid
Capacity 2 Young People (Co-ed)
Bedroom Count 3 Bedrooms (2 Resident, 1 Staff)
  • Ideally positioned close to local schools, GPs, and shopping facilities.
  • Cozy and non-institutional styling designed to feel like a family home.
  • Spacious communal dining areas and private study rooms.
  • Large, safe private outdoor garden.

Suitability & Placement Matching

Willow House is suitable for children and young people aged 8 to 18 who present with emotional and behavioural difficulties (EBD), developmental attachment trauma, or mild learning needs.

Nurturing Growth

Measuring Outcomes & Impact

We track, measure, and evidence tangible developmental outcomes to demonstrate the direct impact of our therapeutic placements.

Educational Engagement

We measure success by increases in school attendance, progress towards EHCP targets, and positive PEP milestones, helping children transition successfully to active learning.

Emotional Wellbeing

Using clinical consultation and regulation tracking, we evidence progress through the child's improved co-regulation skills and reduction in distress or crisis behaviors.

Placement Stability

We focus on building secure attachments to minimize placement breakdowns. We track the longevity of each placement to demonstrate stability.

Partnerships

Why Commissioners Choose Safe Hands

We build strong partnerships with local authorities through responsiveness, clinical governance, and transparent outcomes.

Responsive Referrals

We acknowledge all enquiries within 2 hours and deliver a comprehensive Group Dynamic Matching assessment within 24 hours of receiving the placement document pack.

Rigorous Matching

Our detailed Group Dynamic Impact Assessments protect placement stability by ensuring compatibility between new and existing residents before making any placement offer.
